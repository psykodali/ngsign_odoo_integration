Seamless electronic signatures for your sales workflow

Version 19.0.1.0.0

Overview

Transform your sales process with secure electronic signatures. Send quotations and sales orders directly from Odoo to customers for digital signing through the NGSIGN platform.

Built for efficiency and compliance, this integration provides a professional way to collect legally binding signatures from individuals and company contacts.

Configuration

1

Open Settings

Navigate to Settings ‚Üí General Settings and locate the NGSIGN Integration section.

2

Add Credentials

Enter your NGSIGN API URL and Bearer Token from your NGSIGN account.

3

Save & Start

Click Save to store your configuration. You're ready to send documents for signature.

How to Use

1

Open Sales Order

Create or open a quotation in Draft or Sent state.

2

Send with NGSIGN

Click the Send with NGSIGN button in the sales order header.

3

Select Contact

If the customer is a company, choose which contact should sign and review their details.

4

Confirm

Confirm and send. The contact will receive an email invitation to sign the document.

5

Track

Monitor signature status through transaction UUID, signature URL, and chatter updates.
