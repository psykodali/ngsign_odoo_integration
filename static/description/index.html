NGSIGN Integration for Odoo

Streamline your sales process with secure, legally compliant electronic signatures. The NGSIGN Integration module connects Odoo directly with the NGSIGN platform, enabling you to send quotations and sales orders for digital signing with a single click.

Key Benefits

  • Eliminate printing, scanning, and manual signatures — go 100% digital.
  • Send, track, and retrieve signed documents directly from your Odoo interface.
  • Ensure legally binding, timestamped signatures compliant with international standards.
Ideal for: B2B sales, legal firms, real estate, consulting, and any business that requires secure and fast customer signatures.

Main Features

  • ⚡ One-click sending: Generate and send sales orders for signature instantly.
  • 👥 Smart contact selection: Automatically choose the right contact to sign documents.
  • ✏️ Inline editing: Update contact information before sending for signature.
  • 📋 Flexible templates: Define custom signature positions, pages, and types.
  • 📊 Real-time tracking: Monitor signature status directly from the sales order view.
  • 📥 Automatic attachment: Signed PDFs are automatically downloaded and attached.
  • 🔒 Secure & compliant: Uses certified timestamps and advanced electronic signature methods.

Signature Templates

Create and manage reusable signature templates to handle different document types and signing scenarios.

  • Define precise X/Y coordinates for signatures.
  • Choose between “Last Page” or a specific page for placement.
  • Support for multiple signature types: Simple, DigiGO, or Choose Later.
  • Set default templates for faster document preparation.

How It Works

  1. Configure NGSIGN credentials in Odoo settings.
  2. Create signature templates under NGSIGN → Signature Templates.
  3. Send documents for signature directly from Sales Orders or Quotations.
  4. Track and download signed documents automatically.

Example Workflow

Scenario: Sending a sales quotation to a company contact for signature.

  1. Create a quotation in Odoo and click “Send with NGSIGN”.
  2. Select the appropriate contact (e.g. Procurement Manager).
  3. Confirm the pre-selected template and send for signature.
  4. The recipient signs electronically via NGSIGN.
  5. The signed PDF is automatically retrieved and attached in Odoo.

Advanced Features

  • Resend with full visibility of previous signature attempts.
  • Automatic chatter updates and activity tracking.
  • Color-coded signature status badges (Sent, Signed, Expired).
  • Manual or automatic status synchronization.
  • Multi-company support and customizable templates.
